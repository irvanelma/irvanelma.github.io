Projects

Customer Lifetime Value Predictive Model (Purwadhika Capstone Project 3)

A predictive model for Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) using a Random Forest regressor, aimed at providing businesses with insights into forecasting the long-term value of their customers. By analyzing historical customer data, the model predicts CLV based on a set of customer attributes (Features). A user-friendly interface was developed, which simplifies the application of the model for real-time predictions, making it accessible to non-technical users.

New York Taxi Trip Analysis (Purwadhika Capstone Project 2)

This data analytics project is designed to deliver insights and actionable recommendations for enhancing the fleet management strategies of a taxi company. Utilizing a comprehensive dataset from the New York Taxi & Limousine Commission, this analysis covers detailed records of taxi trips throughout New York City in January 2023. The aim is to identify patterns, inefficiencies, and opportunities within the operational dynamics to improve service delivery and operational efficiency.

Monitoring & Predictive Digital Twin of a Mini Greenhouse

A wireless greenhouse monitoring system utilizing the Arduino IoT Cloud for remote observation via a dedicated app. Implements Machine Learning, specifically Artificial Neural Networks to create a predictive Digital Twin model. This model predicts soil moisture levels based on the greenhouse's specific conditions.

Maze Solver Robot

Autonomous micro-mouse robot aimed to navigate and solve mazes by following the left wall (Left Wall following Algorithm). The Arduino microcontroller and the ultrasonic sensor enables the robot to detect walls to navigate through the labyrinths and performs decision making.
